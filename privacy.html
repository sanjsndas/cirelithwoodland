Privacy Policy

Your privacy is as important to us as a well-tended garden. This policy explains how we care for your personal data.

Last updated: 10 July 2026

At Verdant Verve (referred to as "we", "us", or "our"), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to ensure your data remains secure.

1. Information we collect

We may collect and process the following data about you:

  • Contact details: name, email address, postal address, and phone number that you provide via our contact forms or newsletter sign-ups.
  • Usage data: information about how you use our website, including pages visited and time spent, collected through cookies and similar technologies.
  • Correspondence: records of any communication you send to us, whether via email, social media, or our contact forms.

2. How we use your information

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • To send you gardening tips, seasonal updates, and promotional materials (only with your explicit consent).
  • To improve our website, content, and user experience through analytics.
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud.

🌱 Our promise to you: We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is used only to nurture our relationship with you, just like we nurture our plants.

3. Legal basis for processing

We process your personal data based on one or more of the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: where you have given us explicit permission to use your data (e.g., for marketing communications).
  • Legitimate interests: where processing is necessary for our legitimate interests, such as improving our services, provided your rights do not override those interests.
  • Legal obligation: where we need to comply with a legal or regulatory requirement.

4. Data storage and security

We take data security seriously. We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or alteration. These include encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits.

Your data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law.

5. Your rights

Under the UK GDPR, you have the following rights concerning your personal data:

  • Access: request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure: request that we delete your data in certain circumstances.
  • Restriction: ask us to limit processing of your data.
  • Portability: request a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Objection: object to processing based on legitimate interests or direct marketing.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@verdantverve.com. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. These may include essential cookies (for site functionality), analytics cookies (to understand how you interact with our site), and preference cookies (to remember your settings). You can manage your cookie preferences at any time via our Cookies Policy page.

7. Third-party links

Our website may contain links to other sites (e.g., social media, gardening resources). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal data.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with an updated "last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
