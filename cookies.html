Cookies Policy

Like a garden that thrives with careful tending, our website uses cookies to nurture your browsing experience.

Last updated: 10 July 2026

Verdant Verve ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website. This policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage your preferences.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They help the website recognise your device, remember your preferences, and provide you with a more personalised experience. Cookies are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide analytical information to site owners.

🌿 Our philosophy: We believe in transparency and choice. Just as you choose which plants to grow in your garden, we want you to have control over which cookies you accept. We never use cookies for purposes that compromise your privacy.

Types of cookies we use

We use the following categories of cookies on our website:

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Necessary for the website to function properly. These enable core features such as security, navigation, and form submissions. You cannot opt out of these cookies. Session / Persistent
Analytics Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information about page views, click patterns, and time spent on pages. This helps us improve our content and user experience. Up to 2 years
Preference Remember your settings and choices, such as language preferences, font size, and region, so you don't have to re-enter them each visit. Up to 1 year
Marketing Used to deliver relevant content and advertisements based on your interests. These cookies may be set by third-party providers and may track your browsing activity across different websites. Up to 2 years

Third-party cookies

Some cookies on our website are placed by third-party services we use to enhance our offering. These may include:

  • Google Analytics: To analyse website traffic and user behaviour. Google may use this data in accordance with their own privacy policy.
  • Social media platforms: If you interact with our social media links (e.g., Instagram, Pinterest), these platforms may place cookies on your device.
  • Embedded content: Occasionally, we embed videos or content from platforms like YouTube or Vimeo, which may set their own cookies.

We do not control third-party cookies and recommend you review the privacy policies of these external services.

How to manage your cookie preferences

You can manage or disable cookies at any time through your browser settings. Most web browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete cookies already stored on your device.
  • Block cookies from specific websites or all websites.
  • Set preferences for different types of cookies.

To learn more about managing cookies in your browser, visit the help section of your browser or the following resources:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Your consent

When you first visit our website, we display a cookie banner asking for your consent to set non-essential cookies. By continuing to browse or by clicking "Accept," you agree to our use of cookies as described in this policy. You can change your preferences at any time by adjusting your browser settings or using our cookie preference tool (if available).

Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with an updated "last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
