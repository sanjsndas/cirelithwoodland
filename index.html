    Frequently asked questions

    What is the best time to water?

    Early morning is ideal to reduce evaporation and fungal issues. Deep watering less frequently encourages deep roots.

    How do I improve clay soil?

    Add organic matter (compost, aged manure) and gypsum. Avoid working wet clay to prevent compaction.

    Can I grow vegetables in partial shade?

    Leafy greens, herbs, and root vegetables tolerate 3-4 hours of direct sun. Fruiting plants need 6+ hours.
